Hurricane Florence

South Carolina, North Carolina, Maryland

Hurricane Florence is a powerful storm that is crawling across South Carolina and causing catastrophic flooding across three states. Swansboro, NC has already recieved more than 30 inches of rain; several other cities have more than 20 inches. At 5 p.m. Saturday, the center of the storm was about 60 miles west of Myrtle Beach, South Carolina, moving west at 2 mph. Maximum sustained winds are 45 mph. There are currently 12 people comfirmed dead from the storm related incidents.
